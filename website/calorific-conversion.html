The Calorific Conversion Conquest

We can't go hiking so instead we are going to pretend. By doing exercises at home, we can convert these into a virtual hike. Let's see how far each of us can go, and how far our combined efforts take us!

How does it work?

Head over to the activities page to get yourself into the runnings. First, tell us how many calories a 1km hike would burn for you. Then input your activities and how many calories you burned doing them. We'll figure out the rest and update your score on the leaderboard.
